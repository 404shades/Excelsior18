RULES of
Bioles Events

Biofest Open Quiz

Summary

Biofest is a platform to show your knowledge and prove your skills . Start with written screening test followed by oral and visual rounds for the shortlisted candidates.

RULES

  • It is a group task.
  • Every team has a leader.
  • Every second counts.

Forensic Science Investigations

Summary

Forensic science is the application of science to criminal and civil laws, mainly on criminal side during criminal investigation, as governed by the legal standards of admissible evidence and criminal procedure. Forensic scientist collect , preserve and analyze scientific evidence during the course of investigation.

Rules:

  • This is a group event.
  • Participants team has to collect clues and evidence as much as possible.
  • Crime scene will be created with blood samples and other biological contents.

Humour Your Science

Summary

Biotechnology processes and terms can be the plot of a comic story which can create a scientific humor and a well understanding of matter.

RULES

  • This is solo event.
  • Participants have to create a comic story in accordance to explain a biotech process.

Paper and poster presentation

Summary

A poster presentation is the presentation of research information by an individual or representative of research terms at a congress or conference with a academic or professional focus. A research fellow must know about the paper publishing and appropriate choice of subject matter in his/her research paper.
