Solo Singing

Summary

An event that lets you explore your singing abilities. But this time it?s not just basic singing, you have to keep your instruments or the karaoke ready with you.

Rules

  • Participants must report 30 minutes prior to the event timing.
  • Participants can bring at most two accompanists with them.
  • Time Limit: Time allotted for each performance is 3-5 minutes. A warning bell will be given at the end of 3 minutes.
  • Participants will not be allowed to refer to the lyrics while singing.
  • The participants should bring their own musical instruments or instrumental music (vocals) required in the performance but need to inform the event coordinator beforehand.
  • Every Participant must bring their ID card with them.
  • The participants will be judged on the song selection, voice quality, clarity, rhythm and their appeal to the audience.
  • Choice of Material: Choice of song is open to the participants but the song should not have any slangs or derogatory language.
  • Any obscene/offensive music/song is not allowed.
  • Vulgarity is strongly prohibited. Any form of obscenity will lead to debarring from the contest.
  • The song should not offend any religious sentiments.
  • The decision of the judges would be final and non-disputable.

Solo Dance

Summary

Dance is a performance art form consisting of purposefully selected sequences of human movement. This movement has aesthetic and symbolic value, and is acknowledged as dance by performers and observers within a particular culture.

RULES

  • The participants will be judged on their costumes, visual presentation and dance form.
  • Every Participant must bring their ID card with them.
  • Participants should get their own props. All props must be specified to the event coordinator.
  • Choice of song is open to the participants but not offend any religious sentiments.
  • Any obscene/offensive music/song is not allowed.
  • Vulgarity is strongly prohibited. Any form of obscenity will lead to debarring from the contest.
  • The participants are requested to submit song before the event.
  • Time Limit: Time allotted for each performance is 3-5 minutes.
  • The decision of the judges would be final and non-disputable.

Battle of Bands

Summary

There's something about music that crosses all boundaries and reaches our heart. It's soothing to the mind, it's caressing to the heart. But what happens when you let rock bands from across the country collide in front of a huge audience and add an enormous prize money for the topmost band, their music drives right through your heart. And, that's why we present to you Spectrum, scintillating performances by mega Hindi rock bands, handpicked from across the country, fighting over to win your hearts.

Rules

  • Each team may have at most 6 members.
  • Every Participant must bring their ID card with them.
  • Participants should bring their own guitars/musical instruments.
  • Any obscene/offensive music/song is not allowed. Gestures and Costumes should not be vulgar or there should be no vulgar content, offenders will be disqualified.
  • All band members must refrain from using any offensive forms of communication while on stage.
  • Bands will be judged based on the following criteria: General performance, If the band is well rehearsed ,Song writing (original songs),Song writing ability (originality, creativity, song structure, song dynamics),Technical ability (including range, tone, delivery, rhythm, articulation, etc),Audience connection crowd response.
  • Time Limit: Time allotted for each performance is 15-20 minutes. A warning bell will be given at the end of 15 minutes.
  • The decision of the judges would be final and non-disputable.

Fashionista

Summary

Fashionista is probably one of the events in Excelsior where you can experience the peak of glamour and grace, conviction and jitter, impressive style and intoxicating looks. We bring to you Lifestyle, a fashion show competition filled with glamour and panache.

Rules

  • Every Participant must bring their ID card with them.
  • A Team can have 10-16 members.
  • The team should carry their tracks in pen drive and provide a copy of it to the coordinator.
  • Selection of theme is open for all teams
  • Teams would be judged on confidence, vision and choreography.
  • The dress should not be inappropriate and would be checked by the committee.
  • A green room would be provided for the changing purposes.
  • Vulgarity is strongly prohibited. Any form of obscenity will lead to debarring the team from the contest.
  • Time limit: Time allotted for each performance is 10 min. A warning bell will be given at the end of 8 mins and a final bell will be given at the end of 10 mins.
  • Participants should confirm their presence 30 min before/prior to event.
  • The decision of the judges would be final and non-disputable.
