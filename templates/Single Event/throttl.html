Dual Mobility

Summary

This event needs a boat which is comfortable on land as well as in water for straight line motions and taking turns. An effective way for war in both land and water.

Problem statement:

  • To build an amphibious vehicle that is capable of running on land as well as in water.
  • The vehicle must be able to move on hard surface as well as in water and also be able to steer in water, itâ€™s a mix of racing and overcoming hindrances in minimum time.

Team size: 2-4 members

Rules

  • Machine should be fitted in a box having dimensions 300mm x 300mm x 450mm (L*B*H) at any moment of time during the race (5% tolerance allowed). The external device which is used to control the device is not included in the size constraint.
  • No constraint on weight of robot.
  • The electric voltage anywhere in the machine should not exceed 24 V at any point of time. Power source would be available but you can use battery also.
  • Evaluation will be based on economic feasibility, practical feasibility and innovation.

Cad contest

Summary

Cad contest offers a challenging competition platform to ascertain the best student designer. The competition is a resourceful juncture for the technical players to display their abilities in product designing by applying the principles of physics and material designing and acquire the know-how in the arena which is evaluated by the industry specialists. It will be conducted in single round. 1 hr will be allotted to each participant to design part(s)/ assembly. Evaluation will be based on drawing skills, neatness, completeness and time span.

Mechizmo

Summary

Get ready to take control of your robot while competing with other participants. In this event we provide you a golden opportunity to prove your skill in creating a robot and competing with other participants. This presents a chance for the challenger to show their robotic acumen, intellect and fighter spirit.

Problem statement:

  • To build a robot capable of carrying and placing the cubes (not weighing more than 250 gms) in their places.

Team size: 2-4 members

Rules

  • Robot should be fitted in a box having dimensions 300mm x 300mm x 450mm (L*B*H) at any moment of time during the race (5% tolerance allowed). The external device which is used to control the device is not included in the size constraint.
  • No constraint on weight of robot.
  • The electric voltage anywhere in the machine should not exceed 24 V at any point of time. Power source would be available but you can use battery also.
  • The arm should use only the self-made mechanical mechanisms (Gears, Belt pulleys, Pneumatic, Hydraulic, Cam, Chain-sprocket, etc.).

Evaluation will be based on economic feasibility, practical feasibility and innovation.
