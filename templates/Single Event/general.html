Contraption

Summary

Participants are required to create an imaginative and innovative sequence of energy conversions such that once you provide an initial energy to the setup, the contraption continues to fulfill the final compulsory task, followed by an assortment of steps. 

Compulsory Task in Event: - AIM IS MOVE TO

  • Move the toy car initially.
  • Putting the ball in the dustbin will be Final task.
  • At least five energy conversions must be there in contraption.
  • In the middle of contraption, there should be blocks of wood so that energy will be transferred to the final task.
  • Inflation of the balloon can be done by any needle or any means.

Bonus Task

  • Light a candle.
  • Open a book.
  • Cut an apple with a knife.

RULES AND REGULATIONS

  • There should be a copy of diagram showing energy conversions.
  • Initially 90 minutes will be given for arranging the setup at the event space.
  • You have to bring your own system/setup and Only two trial attempts will be provided.
  • Maximum three hand touches allowed, at 4th ,5th, 6th, hand touches there will be deduction of 5,10,15points respectively.
  • 15 points will be provided for each energy conversion and points for innovation will be decided by judges.
  • Maximum team Members will be 4.
  • 5 marks will be deducted on repeated energy conversion.
  • No power supply will be provided, Toy car will should have its own battery.

JUDGING CRITERIA

  • Number of energy conversions.
  • Type of conversion used Coordination between the Team Members.
  • Recycle products are preferred in energy conversion.1. There should be a copy of diagram showing energy conversions.
  • Originality and Innovation of ideas and overall complexity of contraption.

Rangoli

Treasure Hunt

Summary

It’s a fun-filled teambuilding activity, each team receives a set of clues and where teams need to visit in order to gather more information to solve these clues. The team which solves all set of given clues are the winners of treasure hunt.

Rules

  • The maximum number of participants in a team can be 5.
  • The Teams will have to register themselves prior to participating.
  • Only the first 10 registered teams will be allowed to participate.
  • Usage of any short of unfair means is strictly prohibited.
  • Time duration of this will be 1.5 Hours.
  • The Teams will have to report 30 minutes before the official time of event.
  • Event will be distributed in the University. Use of vehicles is allowed.
  • All the clues of the teams are different and located at different places but the final destination is same.
  • Team must stay together.

Judging Criteria

  • The clues are to be found in a correct order.
  • The Team which will get the Treasure first, with all the clues in correct order will win.

Rubix Cube Challenge

Summary

This is a mind-blowing event, which everyone can attend, true logic and presence of mind is needed in this event by arranging this event we can start a journey to find a genius who can break all the world records by solving the cube in minimum time. Participants will bring their own cubes with them. A group of five participants will take part at a time. Initial stage of all the cubes will be set to the same stage. Time limit-there is no time limit. Person who will solve the cube may report to the volunteers concerned and their time will be recorded.

Rules

  • Participants have to bring their own cube.
  • Maximum of 5 members is allowed to take part at a time.
  • The initial stages of all the cubes will be set same.
  • Participants are requested to notify the volunteers concerned when their cubes are solved so that volunteers may note their time.

  • Out of all the participants three of them who took the least time to solve the cube will be chosen as winners.

No. Of participants

  • There is no limit of the numbers of participants in this event but participants in a group of 5 may be able perform on the stage at a time.

Just A Minute

Summary

Just A Minute or most commonly known as JAM, is sixty seconds of non-stop talking with no repetition, no grammatical errors, no stop and no deviation. Participants will be given a topic on the spot to speak upon.

Rules

  • Contestant will be given a topic to speak on continuously for 60 seconds.
  • There should be no hesitation, no repetition of words except the topic.
  • There should be no grammatical mistakes or deviation from the topic.
  • Points will depend on how many seconds you talk continuously.
  • There will be negative marking by judges for every error they catch.
